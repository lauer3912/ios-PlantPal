Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

PlantPal is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

PlantPal stores all data locally on your device. We collect:

  • Plant information you add (names, photos, care schedules)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Usage data for app performance improvement

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely for:

  • Providing plant care reminders and notifications
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Local data backup and synchronization

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms.

We do not transmit your personal data to any third parties. Your plant collection and personal information never leave your device unless you explicitly export or share them.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete the PlantPal app
  • Request data deletion through our support channels

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your stored plant information within the app
  • Delete: Remove individual plants or all app data at any time
  • Export: Export your data in standard formats for backup

6. Children's Privacy

PlantPal is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Third-Party Services

PlantPal does not contain any advertising, analytics trackers, or third-party services that collect user data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
